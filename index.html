The IET

The Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) is a multidisciplinary professional engineering institution. The IET was formed in 2006 from two separate institutions: the Institution of Electrical Engineers (IEE), dating back to 1871,[1] and the Institution of Incorporated Engineers (IIE) dating back to 1884. Its worldwide membership is currently in excess of 168,000.

Linton IET On Campus

Linton IET was founded by Hovah Yii with the help of Dr. Sadiq & Dr. Ery, friends and committees.

Linton IET welcomes all engineering and IT/ICT students (Mechanical, Civil, Electrical & Electronic, Architecture and also Software engineer) to join Linton IET for their academic performance and career advancement.

Although, the IET is a professional body solely for engineers. Linton IET also welcomes Business, Accounting, Quantity Survey and Graphic Design student to join.

Our Approach

We are a bunch of volunteers and technology geeks who share what we learnt to the other. We work in three ways:

Coach and Teach

Linton IET provides coaching and teaching through bootcamp or club activities. We believe by teaching and coaching one's to grow, he/she can later be the mentor to help others to grow.

Collaboration

We strongly believe in teamwork and collaboration. That's why we teach the students in such a way that they might learn and know how to work and collaborate with others. This can be done by teaching them with certain collaboration skill sets and tools.

Develop and Deliver

We help students to develop their programming skills through coaching and traning. We help you deliver results right through implementation process - including bootcamp and etc.
